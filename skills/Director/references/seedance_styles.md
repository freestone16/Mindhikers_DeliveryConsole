# Seedance 2.0 Style Reference

> **来源**: awesome-seedance & Community Best Practices
> **用途**: 在生成 Prompt 时，根据场景需求选择对应的风格关键词组合。

## 1. 电影质感 (Cinematic)
> **适用**: 深度叙事、情感表达、B-Roll 空镜

- **好莱坞动作**: `Hollywood action movie style, high contrast, teal and orange grading, motion blur, anamorphic lens flare`
- **维伦纽瓦科幻**: `Denis Villeneuve style, minimalist composition, brutalist architecture, desaturated color palette, atmospheric fog, vast scale`
- **王家卫美学**: `Wong Kar-wai style, step printing effect, neon lighting, rain reflection, shallow depth of field, melancholic atmosphere`
- **胶片质感**: `Kodak Portra 400, film grain, vintage texture, warm skin tones, soft lighting`

## 2. 广告/商业 (Commercial)
> **适用**: 产品展示、未来科技感、明亮场景

- **极简品牌**: `MUJI style, clean white background, soft shadows, natural lighting, product focused, 8k resolution`
- **科技宣传**: `Apple commercial style, sleek metallic surfaces, glass reflections, smooth camera movement, studio lighting`
- **奢侈品**: `High-end luxury commercial, golden lighting, silk texture, slow motion, elegant composition`

## 3. 社媒/病毒 (Social & Viral)
> **适用**: Shorts 开头 Hook、快节奏剪辑

- **高饱和**: `High saturation, vibrant colors, pop art aesthetics, eye-catching`
- **TikTok 趋势**: `TikTok viral style, ring light, vertical composition, engaging facial expression`
- **Meme 风格**: `Internet meme aesthetic, exaggerated expression, comic sans text overlay vibe` (需配合 Remotion 文字)

## 4. UGC / 伪纪录片 (Realistic)
> **适用**: 采访、vlog 风格、真实感模拟

- **手机摄影**: `Shot on iPhone, vertical video, handheld camera shake, auto-exposure, natural environment`
- **监控/录像带**: `CCTV footage, low quality, timestamps, night vision, VHS glitch`
- **GoPro**: `GoPro POV, wide angle, fisheye distortion, high action`

## 5. 动漫/动画 (Animation)
> **适用**: 抽象概念解释、二次元受众

- **新海诚**: `Makoto Shinkai style, hyper-detailed clouds, lens flare, emotional scenery, vibrant blue sky`
- **吉卜力**: `Studio Ghibli style, hand-painted background, watercolor texture, pastoral atmosphere`
- **赛博朋克动画**: `Cyberpunk Edgerunners style, neon outlines, chromatic aberration, futuristic city`
- **皮克斯**: `Pixar style, 3D render, subsurface scattering, cute character design, expressiveness`

## 6. 短剧/网剧 (Web Series)
> **适用**: 剧情重演、情感冲突

- **霸总短剧**: `Vertical drama style, high key lighting on face, dramatic closeup, blurry background`
- **悬疑**: `Thriller atmosphere, chiaroscuro lighting, mysterious shadow, dutch angle`

## 7. 视觉特效 (VFX & Abstract)
> **适用**: 脑科学概念、微观世界、心理状态

- **微观**: `Electron microscope view, biology, cells, DNA, detailed texture, shallow focus`
- **抽象流体**: `Abstract 3D fluid simulation, colorful ink in water, slow motion, macro`
- **故障艺术**: `Glitch art, data moshing, digital distortion, cyberpunk aesthetic`
