# Artlist 官方词库协议 (Artlist Vocabulary Protocol)

为了确保搜索的高命中率，当推荐 Artlist 素材时，**必须**优先从以下官方标签中选择，严禁臆造不存在的分类。

## Video Theme (主题)

**常用**:
- `Technology`, `Education`, `Medical & Health`, `Business`
- `Science` (注: 归类于 Professions), `Nature & Landscapes`

**风格**:
- `Abstract`, `Social Aesthetic` (极简/高级感), `VFX Overlay`
- `Animation`, `Time Lapse`

**生活**:
- `Lifestyle`, `Urban`, `Home`, `Travel`, `Transportation`

## Shot Type (镜头与技术)

**景别**:
- `Close-Up`, `Medium Shot`, `Long Shot`, `Macro` (微距)

**运镜**:
- `Smooth Movement`, `Handheld` (手持感), `Tripod` (固定), `Aerials` (航拍)

**光影/时间**:
- `Golden hour`, `Night`, `Day`, `Interior`, `Exterior`

**格式**:
- `4K+`, `Raw & Log`, `Super slow`, `Slow`, `Real time`

## People (人物)

**数量**:
- `No people` (空镜), `One person`, `Two people`, `Group`

**种族/类型**:
- `Asian` (优先), `Caucasian`, `Teenager`, `Child`, `Elderly`, `Business` (职业)
